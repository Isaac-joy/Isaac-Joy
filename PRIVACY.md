# Privacy Policy — Solo Leveling Council

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

This Privacy Policy explains what information the **Solo Leveling Council** app
("the app", "we") collects, how it is used, and your rights. By using the app you
agree to this policy.

## 1. Information we collect
- **Account details:** your email address (for sign-in), managed by our authentication provider.
- **Profile you provide:** name, age, occupation, education level, interests, goals
  (intellectual/financial/physical), body metrics, and available equipment.
- **Content you create:** your daily "Report" logs, missions, workouts, study books/subjects,
  and any text you enter. Your daily logs may contain personal reflections.
- **Activity:** stats, XP, rank, completed quests/chapters, and similar in-app progress.

We do **not** collect precise location, contacts, or device advertising identifiers.

## 2. How we use it — including AI processing
We use your information solely to operate the app's features: to generate the
AI "Council" audits, the "System" verdicts, missions, workouts, study plans, career
guidance, and to track your progression.

**Third-party AI processing:** to generate this content, the relevant text you provide
(e.g. your daily log, goals, profile) is sent to third-party AI providers — **Google
(Gemini / Gemma)** and **OpenRouter** — for processing. These providers process the
content to return a response. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for
advertising.

Data is stored using **Supabase** (database + authentication). The app communicates
with our backend over HTTPS.

## 3. Data sharing
We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only with the service
providers above strictly to provide the app's functionality (AI generation, database,
authentication).

## 4. Your rights — access & deletion
You can **delete your account and all associated data at any time** from within the app
(**Settings → Delete Account**). This permanently removes your profile, logs, and all
in-app data. You may also request deletion by contacting us (below).

## 5. Children
The app is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country).
Do not use the app if you are under that age.

## 6. AI content disclaimer
All "Council", "System", mission, career, and study content is **AI-generated** and is
provided for motivation and general information only. It is **not** professional medical,
financial, legal, career, or psychological advice. Always use your own judgment and
consult a qualified professional where appropriate.

## 7. Changes
We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the date above.
